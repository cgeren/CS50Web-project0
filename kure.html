  • Including the lagoon, the Kure Atoll is .884 km/.341 mi squared.
  • The atoll features a tropical savanna climate, hospitable to many species of birds.
  • In the 1970's, the U.S. Coast Guard set up a station here to ensure the atoll was not
    being used for any nefarious purposes.
  • The atoll has been a popular site for amateur radio use in the past due to it's favorable location.
    in the Hawaiian island chain.
  • Kure Atoll has recently been host to a growing population of Hawaiian monk seals!
