The Diego Garcia Atoll

Return To Home Page

  • Including the lagoon, the Diego Garcia Atoll is 30 km/12 mi squared.
  • The atoll was originally inhabited by French citizens in the late 1700's, who were expelled from the island around 1970.
  • After the expulsion of French settlers, the atoll has been mainly employed by the UK and the US for military purposes.
  • Cocount crabs, hawksbill turtles, leatherback turtles, and even sei whales have been spotted in the atoll!
  • Camp Justice of the United States can be found on Diego Garcia, which doubles as an emergency landing site for all types of commercial aircraft.
