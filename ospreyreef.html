The Osprey Reef Atoll

Return To Home Page

  • The Osprey Reef is a submerged atoll! It covers 195 km/75 mi squared.
  • The atoll has often by quoted to be the "ultimate reef diving adventure".
  • The reef offers tons of "big fish" action due mainly to it being an atoll.
  • The deeper parts are deprived of oxygen, driving larger fish towards the cliffs of the lagoon where oxygen may be found.
  • Osprey Reef is located off of the northeast coast of Australia, 350km north of Cairns.
