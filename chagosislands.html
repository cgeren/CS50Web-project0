The Chagos Islands Atoll

Return To Home Page

  • The exact area of the Chagos Islands Atoll is unknown, but the entire
    archipelago is roughly 56 km/26 square miles.
  • The atoll is a great natural resource for fish and cocounts, which are all shipped to the Diego Garcia Atoll
  • The atoll offers a hot and humid tropical oceanic climate, yet it is
    controlled and moderated by trade winds.
  • The first inhabitants of the Chagos Islands Atoll were presumably lost
    fishermen, who would live off the atoll for days until rescued.
  • Recently, the UK has purchased the atoll for portions of the US military and
    to establish the British Indian Ocean Territory (BIOT).
